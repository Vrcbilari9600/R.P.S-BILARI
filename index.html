About Us

Royall Public School is a symbol of high premium value education and has laid emphasis on the holistic development of the child since its inception in 2021 by Ch. Ram Charan Chandra Charitable Trust.

We emphasize on "High premium quality value education" among the students because we believe that Education is incomplete without placing a high premium on value education so we work upon the young minds and imbue them with idea of love, forgiveness, hard-work, learning, truth, justice, honesty, peace, righteousness, sensitivity, kindness, conduct and service to the society with authentic happiness through these high premium values which help in "HOLISTIC DEVELOPMENT".
